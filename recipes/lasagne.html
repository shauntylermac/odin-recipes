Lasagne!

Home page

This is the most deliciously mouth-watering lasagne you have ever seen

Ingredients

  • 12 Pasta sheets
  • 400g of Tomato sauce
  • 300g of Minced beef

Method

  1. Take your lasagne sheets and place them at the bottom of the pan
  2. Add some of your minced meat mixed with the tomato sauce
  3. Add the cheese that's definitely mentioned in the ingredients list
  4. Bake it until you can't wait anymore, eat with garlic bread.
